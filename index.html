About NorthGate

NorthGate Visa Service Center is an independent visa assistance firm based in Pakistan, providing professional support for visa applications worldwide — with specialized expertise in United States, Canada, Italy, Schengen Countries and beyond.

We are legally registered with SECP & FBR, maintaining strict ethical policies. Our services include end-to-end application support, documentation review, interview preparation, and personalized case handling.
