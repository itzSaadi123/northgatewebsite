Your Student Admission & Visa Journey

01

Student Form submission

Complete and submit your student application form along with basic academic and personal details for assessment.

02

Initial Case Review

Discuss your study goals with our expert counselors and choose the right country, university, and course according to your profile and budget.

03

Profile Evaluation & University Shortlisting

Our admission team carefully reviews your profile and shortlists suitable universities and programs with high approval chances.

04

Admission Processing Fee Submission

Receive your admission processing fee challan/payment details and confirm your application processing with our team.

05

University Admission Application

We prepare and submit your admission applications professionally to the selected universities and institutions.

06

Offer Letter & Admission Confirmation

Receive your Offer Letter / Admission Confirmation from the university college/ academy.

07

Visa File Preparation & Service Fee Submission

Our visa experts prepare your complete visa file, documentation, SOP, financials, and interview guidance after service fee confirmation.

08

Visa Application, Appointment & Tracking

Get your visa appointment scheduled, biometric process completed, and stay updated with real-time application and passport tracking until decision.
